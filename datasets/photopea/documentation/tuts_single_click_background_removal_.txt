# Single-click background removal
Photopea can remove background of a photo with a single click. How can we do it?
Open and press File - Open, to open your photo.
Now, click Select - **Remove BG** , and see the magic happen!
Now, just click File - Export As - PNG, and save it as a PNG file to your computer :)
